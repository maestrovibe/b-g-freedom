About Us

Rooted in agriculture, built for Nigerian farmers

Since our founding, B&G Freedom Nig. Enterprises has worked alongside poultry, fish and cattle farmers to make quality feed and agro-allied products dependable and accessible.

Company Summary

A trusted name in agro-allied supply

B&G Freedom Nig. Enterprises is a trusted agro-allied business dedicated to providing high-quality agricultural products and livestock feed solutions to farmers, agribusinesses and agricultural organisations. We are committed to supporting sustainable agriculture by supplying reliable products that enhance productivity and profitability across the livestock sector.

With a strong focus on quality, affordability and customer satisfaction, we serve poultry, fish and cattle farmers with products designed to promote healthy animal growth and efficient farm operations.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality agro-allied products and nutritious livestock feeds that improve agricultural productivity while delivering exceptional value and service to our customers.

Our Vision

To become one of Nigeria's leading agro-allied enterprises, recognized for quality products, innovation, integrity, and excellence in livestock nutrition.
